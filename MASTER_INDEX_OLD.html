How to Use These Options:

Step 1: Choose your preferred color scheme from the design options above (Black, White, Navy, Gray, Yellow, etc.)
Step 2: Select your preferred text wording from this section
Step 3: Vendor will combine your chosen style + text into final production file

Next Steps

1. Select Design: Choose from Official Building Standards (recommended) or alternative modern designs

2. Board Approval: Present to board and get budget approval (~$230)

3. Vendor Quote: Send specifications to Quick Fast Signs with selected design

4. Production: Vendor produces signs (5-7 business days)

5. Installation: Install signs and begin tracking delivery call reduction
