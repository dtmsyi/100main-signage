Delivery Signage Project - Design Options for Board Review

Project Overview

Problem: Delivery drivers are calling residents approximately 10 times per week asking for package room directions. The current signage creates a "false pathway" where drivers assume the parcel room is inside the lobby when it's actually outside around the corner at the courier door.

Solution: Install clear, explicit signage that prevents intercom calls and directs drivers to the correct exterior entrance.

Goal: Reduce delivery-related resident disruptions by 50%+ within Week 1 of implementation.
