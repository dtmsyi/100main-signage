Step 3 of 4

Select Material

+

Choose the material for all signs. Material affects durability, appearance, and cost. All signs will use the same material.

Recommended Size: 18" x 24" for all signs (consistent, professional appearance)
Pricing: Cost estimates shown below are per sign. Total cost will depend on number of additional signs selected in Step 4.

Aluminum (Recommended)
Durable metal for outdoor use

Premium Aluminum Sign

Professional-grade aluminum with reflective vinyl overlay. Weather-resistant and long-lasting.

Material .063" Aluminum
Finish Reflective vinyl overlay
Durability 10+ years outdoor
Weather Resistance Excellent (rain, sun, snow)
Installation Drill-mounted or adhesive
Size 18" x 24"
Price Per Sign $60-75

Pros

  • Most durable option for outdoor use
  • Professional appearance
  • Reflective finish improves night visibility
  • Won't warp or fade quickly
  • Industry standard for outdoor signage

Cons

  • Higher cost than laminated options
  • Heavier (requires proper mounting)
PVC/Plastic
Lightweight, budget-friendly

Rigid PVC Sign

Rigid plastic board with UV-printed graphics. Lightweight and easy to install.

Material 3mm PVC board
Finish UV-printed, matte or gloss
Durability 3-5 years outdoor
Weather Resistance Good (may fade over time)
Installation Adhesive or lightweight mounting
Size 18" x 24"
Price Per Sign ~$80-90
Based on FastSigns pricing ($35 for 8"x10" scaled up)

Pros

  • Most affordable option
  • Lightweight and easy to install
  • Good for temporary or budget signage
  • Can be cut to custom shapes easily

Cons

  • Less durable than aluminum
  • May warp in extreme temperatures
  • Colors fade faster in direct sunlight
  • Looks less professional
Laminated Vinyl (Economy)
Budget option, shorter lifespan

Laminated Vinyl Decal

Printed vinyl with protective laminate. Can be applied to existing surface.

Material Vinyl with laminate overlay
Finish Matte or gloss laminate
Durability 2-3 years outdoor
Weather Resistance Moderate (may peel/fade)
Installation Self-adhesive (apply to surface)
Size 18" x 24"
Price Per Sign $25-40

Pros

  • Lowest cost option
  • Easy to apply to existing surfaces
  • Can be replaced easily if needed
  • No drilling required

Cons

  • Shortest lifespan (2-3 years)
  • May peel at edges over time
  • Less professional appearance
  • Not reflective (poor night visibility)
  • Fades quickly in direct sunlight
Acrylic (Premium)
High-end, modern appearance

Premium Acrylic Sign

High-quality acrylic with UV-printed or engraved graphics. Modern, sophisticated look.

Material 1/4" thick acrylic
Finish UV-printed or laser engraved
Durability 7-10 years outdoor
Weather Resistance Excellent (UV resistant)
Installation Standoffs or adhesive mounting

Pros

  • Most premium, sophisticated appearance
  • Excellent durability and UV resistance
  • Modern, high-end look
  • Won't rust or corrode
  • Easy to clean

Cons

  • Highest cost option
  • Can crack if impacted
  • May not match rustic building aesthetic

Recommendation:

Aluminum with reflective vinyl is the industry standard for outdoor signage and offers the best balance of durability, visibility, and professional appearance. For a ~$230 budget, aluminum is the recommended choice.
