Design Options Matching Existing Building Signage

Design Philosophy: Consistency with Existing Aesthetic

These design variations match the exact visual style of current 100 & Main signage, ensuring brand consistency and cohesive building aesthetics.

Why This Matters: Using signage styles that already exist in the building creates visual harmony and professional consistency. Residents and visitors will recognize these as "official" building signage, lending authority to the delivery instructions.

Reference: Existing Building Signage Analyzed

Style A: Gray + White Border

  • Background: Medium gray (#6B6B6B)
  • Border: White, thick frame
  • Text: White, sans-serif, spaced
  • Arrow: Large, clean, white
  • Seen on: Mailroom directional sign

Style B: Aluminum Plaque

  • Material: Brushed aluminum look
  • Text: White, engraved appearance
  • Finish: Metallic gradient
  • Border: Subtle beveled edge
  • Seen on: Package delivery door sign

Style C: Minimal White

  • Background: Clean white
  • Text: Bold black, simple
  • Border: None or minimal
  • Style: Minimalist, modern
  • Seen on: Parking space numbers

Recommendation: Building-Consistent Design

Top Recommendation: Style 1 (Gray + White Border) - This matches your existing "PARCEL/PACKAGE DELIVERY" sign aesthetic perfectly and creates immediate visual association with official building signage.

Benefits of Matching Existing Style:
  • Drivers recognize it as official building signage (not temporary or homemade)
  • Visual consistency across all building wayfinding
  • Professional, cohesive aesthetic
  • No aesthetic approval concerns from board
  • Can reuse same design for future signage needs

Material Recommendation: Use the same material/finish as your existing signs for perfect consistency. Ask Quick Fast Signs what material was used for the original "PARCEL/PACKAGE DELIVERY" sign and request the same.
